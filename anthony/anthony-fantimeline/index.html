Lionel Messi

Lionel Messi is an argentine professional footballer who plays as a forward for FC Barcelona and the Argentina national team.

Birthday (1987)

Lionel Messi was born on June 24th, 1987 in Rosario, Argentina.

First Team(1994-2004)

Lionel Messi's career began with the Newell's Old Boys.

First Goal for Barcelona (2005)

Lionel Messi scored his first goal for Barcelona on May 1, 2005.

First Ballon d'Or(2009)

Lionel Messi won his first Ballon d'Or in 2009.

500th Goal for Barcelona(2017)

Lionel Messi scored his 500th career goal on April 23, 2017 against Real Madrid.
